Butternut Squash Soup

Home

Description

Looking for dinner ideas? Why not butternut squash soup! This is butternut squash soup, the perfect comfort food for a cold autumn evening.

Butternut squash soup is easy to make and is so creamy and delicious it is sure to please.

Ingredients

  • 3lbs butternut squash
  • Yellow onion and garlic
  • Fresh sage and rosemary
  • Ginger
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Vegetable broth
  • Salt and freshly-ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Chop the veggies! Saut√© the onion until it becomes translucent, add the squash, and cook until it begins to soften
  2. Stir in the herbs, garlic, and ginger. Pour in the broth and simmer until the squash becomes tender, about 20 minutes.
  3. When the squash is soft, transfer the soup to a blender. Blend until the soup is vibrant orange and creamy
  4. Pour it into bowls, garnish with chopped parsley and pepitas.
  5. Enjoy!
