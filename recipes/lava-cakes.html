Molten Lava Cakes

Home

Description

Tasty, hot lava cakes full of warmth and love (and chocolate)

Ingredients

  • 4 melted semi-sweet chocolate
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 2 egg

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°
  2. Melt chocolate in a bowl nested in hot water
  3. combine butter and sugar
  4. add eggs and whisk until foaming
  5. add dry ingredients
  6. gently fold in chocolate
  7. split into two rammekin
  8. bake for 11 minutes
